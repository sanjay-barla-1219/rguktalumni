Welcome to the Alumni Page of Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies, Srikakulam (RGUKT Srikakulam). Here, we celebrate and connect with our esteemed alumni who have contributed significantly to various fields and made a positive impact on society.

As an RGUKT Srikakulam alumnus, you are an integral part of our extended family. This platform aims to foster a vibrant and enduring alumni community, allowing you to reconnect with fellow graduates, explore new opportunities, and contribute to the growth of our alma mater.

On this page, you will find a wealth of resources and opportunities tailored specifically for our alumni. Stay updated on the latest news and events, including reunions, workshops, and seminars. Connect with your fellow alumni through our alumni directory, where you can discover and network with professionals from diverse backgrounds.

We take immense pride in the achievements of our alumni. We highlight their success stories, showcasing their accomplishments, career paths, and contributions to their respective fields. By sharing these inspiring narratives, we aim to inspire and motivate current students, creating a strong sense of pride and connection within the RGUKT Srikakulam community.

RGUKT Srikakulam's commitment to excellence extends beyond graduation. We encourage alumni to stay engaged and contribute to the growth of the university. Share your expertise by becoming a mentor to current students, participating in career development programs, or collaborating on research and innovation initiatives. Your valuable insights and support play a crucial role in shaping the next generation of leaders and innovators.

We invite you to explore this alumni page, reconnect with your peers, and leverage the vast opportunities and resources available to you. Let us celebrate your achievements, inspire others, and continue the legacy of excellence that defines RGUKT Srikakulam.

Thank you for being an integral part of our alumni community, and we look forward to your continued involvement and success. Together, we can create a lasting impact and shape a brighter future.
